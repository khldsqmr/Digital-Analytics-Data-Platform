Hi team — I’ve finalized the TSD Gold layer.

`vw_sdi_tsd_gold_unified_daily`: wide daily Gold at `event_date + lob + channel` with conformed Adobe, SA360, GSC, Platform Spend, and GMB metrics for base reporting.

`vw_sdi_tsd_gold_unifiedMonSun_weekly`: wide weekly Gold at `weekMonToSun + lob + channel` with the same non-ProFound metrics rolled to Monday–Sunday weeks for standard weekly reporting.

`vw_sdi_tsd_gold_unifiedSunSat_weekly`: wide weekly Gold at `weekSunToSat + lob + channel` with the same non-ProFound metrics rolled to Sunday–Saturday weeks for QGP-style reporting.

`vw_sdi_tsd_gold_unified_monthly`: wide monthly Gold at `monthStart + lob + channel` with the same non-ProFound metrics rolled to monthly grain.

`vw_sdi_tsd_gold_profound_weekly`: wide weekly Gold at `period_date + lob + channel` with ProFound/GoFish AI Search citation share and visibility score metrics.

`vw_sdi_tsd_gold_profound_monthly`: wide monthly Gold at `period_date + lob + channel` with ProFound/GoFish AI Search citation share and visibility score metrics.

`vw_sdi_tsd_gold_long`: long Gold at `data_source + time_granularity + date + lob + channel + metric_name` for flexible metric-level reporting and dashboarding.
