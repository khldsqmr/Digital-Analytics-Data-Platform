-- QUERY: Offer type volume by CARRIER, split across 3 weeks
-- Purpose: Same comparison as before, but now broken out so T-Mobile,
-- Verizon, and AT&T each get their own column per week — lets you see
-- whose offer volume actually drove the 07-11 (Prime Day week) spike,
-- rather than one blended total across all carriers.
-- Source: offerCountsByCarrierAndType_weekly
-- ============================================================================
SELECT
  offer_type,

  -- T-Mobile
  SUM(CASE WHEN carrier = 'T-Mobile' AND report_date = '2025-07-03' THEN offer_count ELSE 0 END) AS tmo_0703,
  SUM(CASE WHEN carrier = 'T-Mobile' AND report_date = '2025-07-11' THEN offer_count ELSE 0 END) AS tmo_0711,
  SUM(CASE WHEN carrier = 'T-Mobile' AND report_date = '2025-07-18' THEN offer_count ELSE 0 END) AS tmo_0718,

  -- Verizon
  SUM(CASE WHEN carrier = 'Verizon' AND report_date = '2025-07-03' THEN offer_count ELSE 0 END) AS vzw_0703,
  SUM(CASE WHEN carrier = 'Verizon' AND report_date = '2025-07-11' THEN offer_count ELSE 0 END) AS vzw_0711,
  SUM(CASE WHEN carrier = 'Verizon' AND report_date = '2025-07-18' THEN offer_count ELSE 0 END) AS vzw_0718,

  -- AT&T
  SUM(CASE WHEN carrier = 'AT&T' AND report_date = '2025-07-03' THEN offer_count ELSE 0 END) AS att_0703,
  SUM(CASE WHEN carrier = 'AT&T' AND report_date = '2025-07-11' THEN offer_count ELSE 0 END) AS att_0711,
  SUM(CASE WHEN carrier = 'AT&T' AND report_date = '2025-07-18' THEN offer_count ELSE 0 END) AS att_0718

FROM `ds_dbi_digitalmedia_automation.sdi_competitiveOffers_insights_offerCountsByCarrierAndType_weekly`
WHERE report_date IN ('2025-07-03', '2025-07-11', '2025-07-18')
  AND carrier IN ('T-Mobile', 'Verizon', 'AT&T')
GROUP BY offer_type
ORDER BY tmo_0711 + vzw_0711 + att_0711 DESC;

The dates match almost exactly. Prime Day 2025 was confirmed as a 4-day event, July 8–11. It was a bigger event than usual. This was Prime Day's 10th anniversary, extended to 4 days for the first time ever (previously 2 days), with Amazon explicitly ramping up the promotion — plus a full week of lead-up deals starting July 1-3. Prime Day 2024 alone drove $14.2 billion in U.S. consumer spend per Adobe Analytics.

=== NOTES FOR THE TEAM ===

- Every table is keyed by report_date (weekly snapshot) — join across
  tables on that column for cross-table analysis.
- carrierOfferGrid and postpaidDeviceOffers are the two tables that share a
  genuine carrier x item x offer shape and are the source for every insight
  table — start there for most competitive-offer questions.
- offer_amount_numeric (a parsed float) is the column to use for any
  aggregate math (AVG, SUM, etc.) — offer_amount is the display string
  (e.g. "$800 off") and isn't safe to aggregate directly.
- majorOfferChanges is the only table with a segment (postpaid/prepaid)
  column directly — for segment cuts on other tables, join to
  control_knownCarriers on carrier = carrier_name.
- Data currently spans 2025-07-03 through 2025-09-05 (10 weekly reports).
