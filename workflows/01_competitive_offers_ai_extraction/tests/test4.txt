=== CORE GOLD TABLES (one per page type) ===

`ds_dbi_digitalmedia_automation.sdi_competitiveOffers_gold_ciHeadlines_weekly`
  What it holds: Narrative CI headline bullets per carrier
  Key columns: report_date, carrier, section, headline_title, headline_detail, has_changes

`ds_dbi_digitalmedia_automation.sdi_competitiveOffers_gold_nationalRetailReadout_weekly`
  What it holds: Best Buy / Walmart retail promo call-outs
  Key columns: report_date, retailer, device, discount_amount, carrier_service

`ds_dbi_digitalmedia_automation.sdi_competitiveOffers_gold_majorOfferChanges_weekly`
  What it holds: Week-over-week diff — what was introduced/removed
  Key columns: report_date, segment, carrier, change_type, description, has_change

`ds_dbi_digitalmedia_automation.sdi_competitiveOffers_gold_voicePlanComparison_weekly`
  What it holds: Business voice plan pricing matrix (Good/Better/Best)
  Key columns: report_date, lines, tier, carrier, plan_name, price

`ds_dbi_digitalmedia_automation.sdi_competitiveOffers_gold_businessInternetComparison_weekly`
  What it holds: Business internet plan spec/pricing comparison
  Key columns: report_date, carrier, plan_tier, metric_name, metric_value

`ds_dbi_digitalmedia_automation.sdi_competitiveOffers_gold_prepaidPriceGridDetail_weekly`
  What it holds: Metro by T-Mobile phone price grid
  Key columns: report_date, device_name, connection_type, plan_price_tier, device_price

`ds_dbi_digitalmedia_automation.sdi_competitiveOffers_gold_prepaidAccessoryPriceGrid_weekly`
  What it holds: Metro watch/tablet/gateway/hotspot pricing
  Key columns: report_date, device_name, device_category, ban_status, price

`ds_dbi_digitalmedia_automation.sdi_competitiveOffers_gold_postpaidServiceOffers_weekly`
  What it holds: Narrative service-level features (BYOD, 5G news, etc.)
  Key columns: report_date, carrier, category, feature_name, detail

`ds_dbi_digitalmedia_automation.sdi_competitiveOffers_gold_carrierOfferGrid_weekly`   [LARGEST TABLE]
  What it holds: BTS / cable MVNO / prepaid brand offer grid
  Key columns: report_date, page_type, carrier, item_name, plan_tier, offer_type, offer_amount, offer_amount_numeric

`ds_dbi_digitalmedia_automation.sdi_competitiveOffers_gold_postpaidDeviceOffers_weekly`   [2ND LARGEST TABLE]
  What it holds: Postpaid smartphone/tablet trade-in offers (Apple, Samsung/Google, Motorola, Business)
  Key columns: report_date, device_category, carrier, item_name, plan_tier, offer_type, offer_amount, offer_amount_numeric


=== INSIGHT TABLES (pre-aggregated, built only from carrierOfferGrid + postpaidDeviceOffers) ===

`ds_dbi_digitalmedia_automation.sdi_competitiveOffers_insights_offerCountsByCarrier_weekly`
  What it holds: Total offer count, distinct devices, distinct offer types — per carrier per week

`ds_dbi_digitalmedia_automation.sdi_competitiveOffers_insights_offerCountsByCarrierAndType_weekly`
  What it holds: Offer count broken down by offer_type (discount, trade-in credit, bogo, etc.)

`ds_dbi_digitalmedia_automation.sdi_competitiveOffers_insights_offerCountsByDevice_weekly`
  What it holds: Offer count + distinct carrier count per device — good for "who's competing on what"

`ds_dbi_digitalmedia_automation.sdi_competitiveOffers_insights_offerCountsByCarrierAndDevice_weekly`
  What it holds: Cross-tab of carrier x device offer counts

`ds_dbi_digitalmedia_automation.sdi_competitiveOffers_insights_offerAmountStatsByCarrier_weekly`
  What it holds: avg/min/max dollar offer amount per carrier per week


=== REFERENCE / CONTROL TABLES (not analytical, but useful context) ===

`ds_dbi_digitalmedia_automation.sdi_competitiveOffers_control_knownCarriers`
  Purpose: Carrier name -> segment (postpaid/prepaid/cable_mvno) mapping — useful for JOINs to group by segment

`ds_dbi_digitalmedia_automation.sdi_competitiveOffers_control_offerTypeCategories`
  Purpose: The canonical offer-type taxonomy (discount, trade-in credit, etc.)


=== NOTES FOR THE TEAM ===

- Every table is keyed by report_date (weekly snapshot) — join across
  tables on that column for cross-table analysis.
- carrierOfferGrid and postpaidDeviceOffers are the two tables that share a
  genuine carrier x item x offer shape and are the source for every insight
  table — start there for most competitive-offer questions.
- offer_amount_numeric (a parsed float) is the column to use for any
  aggregate math (AVG, SUM, etc.) — offer_amount is the display string
  (e.g. "$800 off") and isn't safe to aggregate directly.
- majorOfferChanges is the only table with a segment (postpaid/prepaid)
  column directly — for segment cuts on other tables, join to
  control_knownCarriers on carrier = carrier_name.
- Data currently spans 2025-07-03 through 2025-09-05 (10 weekly reports).
